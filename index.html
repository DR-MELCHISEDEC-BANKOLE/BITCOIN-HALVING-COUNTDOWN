Bitcoin halving is a critical event in its blockchain, occurring approximately every four years (210,000 blocks).
Bitcoin halvings are associated with specific block heights, with the 2024 halving expected at block height 840,000.
The halving cycle occurs every 210,000 blocks, shaping the supply dynamics of Bitcoin.
The last halving was in May 2020, and the next is expected around April 2024, reducing the block reward from 6.25 to 3.125 bitcoins.
